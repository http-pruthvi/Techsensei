# TechSensei - Hackathon Presentation Content (3 Slides)

---

## Slide 1: The Problem & Our Solution

**The Challenge**: 
Developers and students face massive **information overload** and **complexity** in modern tech. "Tutorial hell," outdated documentation, and lack of personalized guidance make learning inefficient and building difficult.

**The Solution: TechSensei**
An **AI-Powered Learning Assistant & Productivity Tool** that transforms how you learn and build.
*   **Adaptive Learning**: Explanations tailored to your exact skill level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced).
*   **Context-Aware**: It doesn't just chat; it *understands* your specific codebase and project context.
*   **Unified Workflow**: A single platform to learn concepts, visualize architecture, and get real-time debugging help while you build.

---

## Slide 2: Tech Stack & Key Innovations

**Core Features**
1.  **Adaptive Explanation Engine**: Generates visual diagrams and text based on your learning style (Visual/Textual).
2.  **Smart Code Analysis**: Instantly parses GitHub repos to visualize dependencies and structure.
3.  **Knowledge Vault**: Automatically organizes what you learn into a searchable personal library.

**Architecture (Scalable & AI-Driven)**
*   **Frontend**: React + TypeScript + Vite for a high-performance UI.
*   **Backend**: Serverless Functions (AWS Lambda ready) for event-driven scaling.
*   **AI Engine**:
    *   **LLMs (e.g., Claude/Titan)** for natural language reasoning.
    *   **Vector DB (Pinecone)** for semantic search (RAG).
    *   **Tree-sitter** for precise code structure analysis.

## Slide 3: Impact, Business Potential & Future

**Market Opportunity**
*   **Target**: Millions of CS students, bootcamp grads, and enterprise dev teams.
